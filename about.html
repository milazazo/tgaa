The Great Ace Attorney Chronicles Art Collaboration
Main • About • Artist
To celebrate the 2 year anniversary of the English release of The Great Ace Attorney: Chronicles, we are hosting an art collaboration featuring every character in the game! This collaboration will be open to all artists no matter the skill level or experience; all we ask is that you are able to send a full-body drawing with transparent background.

The final collaboration will be hosted on this website where you'll be able to see all of the artwork together. You can click on the individual pieces to view them at full size and see artist info. We will also share the final collab as either an image or video on Twitter. Let's enjoy the annivesary of TGAA together!

The information on this page is also available as a Google document.

Sign-up Information

✧ Sign-ups will open April 1st at 3 PM PST.
✧ Sign-ups are first come-first served and capped at 51 artists. Sign-ups will close on April 30th or when 50 forms are received.
✧ While sign-ups themselves are chosen by whoever fills the form first, character assignments are not. Assigned character(s) will only be sent via email at the end of the sign-up period.
✧ When you fill the form, you can write at least 8 characters you would be interested in drawing. You can also choose if you'd be OK with drawing any character or multiple characters (in case there aren't enough artists by the end of the sign-up period).
⠀⠀⠀⠀✧ Please include at least 3 'minor characters' in your list! We're expecting most people will want to draw the 'main characters', but we can only accept one artist per character! If you could be as broad as possible with which characters you'd like to draw, that would be the best.
⠀⠀⠀⠀✧ Note that if you choose 'OK with drawing any character', you may be chosen to draw any of the characters shown here.
⠀⠀⠀⠀✧ In the case that we are unable to assign you to any of the characters you listed, we will check if you are OK with drawing another available character.

Submission Information

✧ Once the sign-up period ends, we will email you your assigned character.
✧ Sign-ups are first come-first served and capped at 51 artists. Sign-ups will close on April 30th or when 50 forms are received.
✧ We will send an email occasionally to remind you of the project, however there will be no formal check-in (you do not need to send WIPs unless you want).
⠀⠀⠀⠀✧ There will be an optional Discord server which you can join if you want to meet others drawing for the collaboration, however it is not a requirement.
✧ You have to send the final image by June 30. Image requirements:
⠀⠀⠀⠀✧ Full-body drawing of the character in their 'regular' outfit
⠀⠀⠀⠀✧ At least 1000×1000px and in color. Doesn't need to be fully shaded
⠀⠀⠀⠀✧ PNG format with transparent background
✧ In case you need to drop out of the project or need some extra time, please let us know immediately so we can organize a backup.

Questions

What will the website be like?
All completed images will be viewable on the main page. You'll be able to click on the image to view it in full size and see artist info. The site will be revamped more in the following months to improve code and visuals. For examples of other collab project websites, see: Christmas in London, Gyakuten Crossover, Ace Attorney Fairytale, and Gyakuten Halloween (all AA examples; the formats are a bit different from our goal but it'll be something like these).

Who can join?
Any artist can join, as long as you are able to fulfil the requirement of sending a full-body with transparent background! Previous experience is not required and 'skill level' will not be taken into account. However, we will still ask for previous art samples in order to assign characters better (so that the old man likers can draw old men basically).

What if not all slots are filled by the end of the sign-up period?
If there are enough artists who are OK with drawing multiple characters, we will proceed with featuring all characters. If there aren't enough artists for that, we will downscale and focus on major characters/major side characters only.

What about characters with multiple 'regular' outfits?
You can pick either 'regular' outfit you want (e.g. Asogi's school uniform or prosecutor suit are both OK, Gina's thief or detective outfit are both OK). Please don't draw characters in their DLC costumes or any other outfits aside from what they usually wear.

Who is the organizer?
It's run by Andrew (milaza_zo/milaza_dgs). I keep saying we to sound more professional but it's just me. Hi. Favorite TGAA character is Van Zieks (note: I won't be drawing him for this event; I'll take whichever character is left over). I'm extra and like coding websites which is why it's like this instead of a digital zine.
