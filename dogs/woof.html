DGS: The DGS Dogboy Zine!

About

DOGS is a Dai Gyakuten Saiban (The Great Ace Attorney) fanbook all about the cast as adorable cute puppy doggies. It featured 34 artists and was available for PDF download from February 10th to March 3rd. In this time, it raised $1074 NZD ($664 USD) for the charity Blind Low Vision NZ (Guide Dogs). Thank you to everyone who supported this zine!

On this page, you can see a low-res gallery of the artwork featured in the zine, as well as some brand-new colored versions!
