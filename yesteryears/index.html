yesteryears

A Dai Gyakuten Saiban anthology featuring the characters of London 10 years prior.

About

yesteryears focuses on London as it was 10 years prior to the storyline of DGS. It will include characters such as Barok, Klint, Lady Baskerville, Genshin, Herlock, Yujin, Seishiro, Albert, Courtney, Esmerelda, Enoch, etc. It is split into two physical A5 zines:

  • A full-color illustration zine, estimated 24~32 pages.
  • A B/W comic and fic anthology, estimated 80~100 pages.
Fluff, angst, humor, whatever; as long as it involves the cast of London from a decade ago, it fits. This anthology is PG-13 (same content level as games).

Roles

  • [1~2] Cover artist: Full color.
  • [15~25] Illust artist: Full color. Can do single page or spread.
  • [5~15] Comic artist: B/W only. Can do a comic from 2~12 pages in length.
  • [5] Fic writer: Maximum length is 3000 words.
